About Shree Hari Agencies

Shree Hari Agencies is a leading commission-based agency that connects India's largest retail stores with the best suppliers in Mumbai. With years of experience in the supply chain and logistics industry, we have built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and efficiency.

Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to ensure that our clients receive the best products at competitive prices, while maintaining the highest standards of quality and service. We pride ourselves on our ability to understand the unique needs of each client and provide customized solutions that drive business growth.

Our Business Model

Shree Hari Agencies operates as a strategic intermediary between India's largest retail stores and top-tier suppliers in Mumbai. Our business model is designed to create a win-win situation for all parties involved, ensuring efficiency, quality, and competitive pricing.

1

Connecting Retailers

We partner with large retail stores across India, understanding their specific product requirements, quality standards, and delivery expectations.

2

Supplier Identification

Our team identifies and vets the best suppliers in Mumbai who can meet the retailers' requirements in terms of quality, quantity, and timeline.

3

Negotiation & Coordination

We handle all negotiations regarding pricing, terms, and conditions, while coordinating logistics to ensure smooth operations.

4

Quality Assurance

Our quality control team ensures that all products meet the agreed standards before they are shipped to the retail stores.

5

Commission-Based Service

We operate on a commission basis, earning our revenue based on successful transactions, which aligns our interests with those of our clients.

6

Continuous Improvement

Regularly review our processes and relationships to identify opportunities for improvement and innovation in the supply chain.
